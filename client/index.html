What is Axion Sword?

A fun and casual entry point into the art of the Sword

Axion Sword fuses the philosophy, physicality, and fun of traditional styles of swordsmanship into a 21st century sport. Foam sword is a fun, high paced, exciting, and safe way for people of all abilities to express their inner Jedi and experience the exhilaration, intensity, and intelligence of sword fighting.

Our Journey

This is how everything began

  • The foundation of our practice

    Dae Han Mu Do Gwan (1997 - 2010)

    In a quiet suburb in Washington Sung Baek 75th Grandmaster of Dong Han (Eastern Scholars) Clan, a Korean lineage that specializes in swordsmanship and medicine, trained a group of dedicated students in the traditional methods of qigong, iaido, kendo and acupuncture.

  • Success in martial arts society

    World Swordsman Federation (2009)

    In 2009, At the United States Kuo Shu Federation Tournament held in Baltimore, MD, we created the World Swordsman Federation winning gold, silver, and bronze medals. Through competing with various martial arts schools we saw the great potential of a sport where practitioners of different styles could safely spar in a spirit of friendly competition.

  • Sharing knowledge with healers

    Daosource (2012)

    In 2012 Master Jonah Chin, Master Lina Kim, Master Ken Kang expanded on the successes of the World Swordsmanship Federation, this time sharing our system of healing with a wide variety of health professionals through a project called Daosource. Daosource provided retreats and seminars focusing on qigong and oriental medicine throughout the US and Canada.

  • Opening our doors to the public

    Qi Club (기회) (2015)

    In 2014 Daosource team moved to Victoria, BC where a group of dedicated practitioners, including Jesse Cohen, partnered with Oshio College of Acupuncture to train and study. We built the Qi Club, a 3,000 sq ft healing center, to share our practices with the general public for the first time.

  • Creating a community

    Urban Temple (2016)

    In 2015, we made the move to San Francisco founding the Urban Temple where we are connecting with the cutting edge visionaries of the city while evolving our deeply rooted traditional practices to help provide solutions to the unique challenges of today’s generation.

  • Healing through having fun

    Axion Sword (2016 - present)

    Through our experiences we learned one of the most effective remedies to the stresses and struggles of city life is the combination of sweat, self-expression, and fun. Axion Sword is a new sport where we spar for the mutual betterment of our partner; cultivating focus, flow, and the freedom from our self-imposed limitations.
